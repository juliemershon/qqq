Why QQQ?

Our Story

I was inspired to create a tailor-made emergency food supply during the California earthquake swarm of 2019. Initially, it was just a generic idea for emergency food kits for the people of California. However, since we're all a picky bunch with crazy dietary needs, I knew I needed to cater to that kind of crowd... everything from its contents and style, to design and presentation.

Some companies are already in the business of making foods for dietary needs, but all of them lack the presentation to really sell a chi-chi (shee shee) Californian on the idea of actually preparing for an emergency. Basically, nearly all competitor sites cater to the doomsday-redneck-crowd banking on a full government meltdown in order to have a bullet-frenzied free-for-all on the general public... and that's just not our style on the west coast.

Not only that, I like the idea of sustainability mixed in... everything from providing seeds to make a home garden, to eco-friendly or reusable packaging, to general sensibility when it comes to longevity. With all that in mind, perhaps you can see why I made Qalifornia Quake Qits.
